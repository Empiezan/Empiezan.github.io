Who am I?

I am a software engineer driven by desire to develop software that helps my peers or does cool things.
In middle school, I loved to create TI-83, TI-84, and TI-89 programs that made me and my classmates' homework easier. I shared all the programs I made on ticalc.org. Now, I am developing web applications to help the Washington University Chemistry Tournament perform live scoring during the Breaking Bonds exam and facilitate the grading of the Individual, Topics, and Team exams. Check out my latest product for the WashU Chemistry Tournament, QR-Grader!
